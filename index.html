About

applied to the cloud.

I am Vivian Chiamaka Okose -- a DevOps engineer with a background in biochemistry and biotechnology. I bring the same rigour I applied to laboratory research to the infrastructure systems I build.

My focus is agentic DevOps: designing AI-powered pipelines where Claude Code handles execution, Terraform manages infrastructure, and safety hooks ensure nothing irreversible runs without deliberate approval.

I believe safety is architecture, not discipline. Every system I build has guardrails designed in from the start -- minimal permissions, read-only auditors, plan-before-apply gates.

Based in Nigeria. Available for remote DevOps engineering, cloud infrastructure consulting, and AI automation projects.
